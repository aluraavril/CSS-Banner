Turn Ons
When looking for a partner...
  • I look for someone who is intelligent and engaging.
  • A great sense of humor is incredibly appealing to me.
  • Kindness towards others is a quality I admire.
  • I'm impressed by someone with ambition to succeed.
  • Confidence is a trait that I find very attractive.
Turn Offs
When looking for a partner...
  • I'm turned off by arrogance and a sense of entitlement.
  • Dishonesty is a major dealbreaker for me.
  • I'm put off by a lack of ambition to improve.
  • Negativity is something I try to avoid in a partner.
  • I find a lack of empathy to be a huge turn-off.
